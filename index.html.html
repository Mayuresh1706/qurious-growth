// What We Do

COMPLETE END-TO-END
PODCAST MANAGEMENT

Every moving part of your podcast — from the first outreach email to the last reel — handled with precision.

01 / 06
📨
GUEST OUTREACH

We don't just find guests — we engineer conversations worth having. Our outreach process turns cold leads into confirmed bookings through strategy, precision, and persistence.

  • We build a curated guest list perfectly aligned with your show's niche and audience, plus a dream guest list of aspirational names worth shooting for
  • Every outreach message is crafted personally for each guest — no mass templates, no spam — just compelling, relevant pitches that get replies
  • We send structured follow-ups to keep conversations warm and convert interest into confirmed recording dates
  • We leverage your existing network strategically, identifying hidden gems in your connections who can add serious credibility to your show
02 / 06
🔍
GUEST RESEARCH

Mediocre interviews come from surface-level prep. We go deep so every conversation feels tailor-made for your listener.

  • In-depth research on each guest — their journey, ideas, controversies, blind spots, and what they've never been asked before
  • We step into the shoes of your ideal listener and design questions they'd actually want answered
  • Questions are then optimised and adapted for the specific platform — long-form depth for podcasts, punchy angles for clips and reels
  • Pre-call briefs delivered to you so you walk into every recording confident, curious, and ready
03 / 07
🚀
MULTI-PLATFORM DISTRIBUTION

One recording. A month's worth of content. We extract maximum reach from every episode and push it across the platforms where your audience already lives.

  • Full podcast published on YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts — properly formatted, tagged, and optimised for discoverability
  • Vertical reels and short clips for Instagram & YouTube Shorts — cut to hook-first formats that stop the scroll
  • Designed carousels for LinkedIn — professional, insight-rich slides that drive saves and shares
  • X (Twitter) threads that distil the episode's best ideas into quotable, shareable formats
  • Newsletter write-ups for LinkedIn and email that deepen audience loyalty
04 / 07
🎁
GUEST SOCIAL MEDIA KIT

When your guest shares the episode, it multiplies your reach overnight. We make it effortless for them by delivering a ready-to-post content kit the moment the episode goes live.

  • Custom-designed graphics and clips tailored to the guest's personal brand and platform presence
  • Pre-written LinkedIn posts, X threads, and Instagram captions written in the guest's voice — ready to copy-paste
  • A personalised episode summary they can share with their own audience and newsletter
  • When guests promote with ease, their audience becomes your audience — compounding your reach with every release
05 / 07
🎬
EDITING & ENGAGING TRAILERS

Your audio and video should sound and look as sharp as the ideas inside them. We edit every episode to broadcast quality — and open each one with a trailer that earns the listen.

  • Full episode editing — silence removal, filler cuts, audio levelling, music, and transitions done with care
  • A punchy trailer placed at the top of each episode gives your audience a taste of what's coming and builds immediate anticipation
  • Trailers double as standalone promo content for social platforms and pre-launch marketing
  • Dynamic captions, b-roll suggestions, and visual formatting for video podcast versions
06 / 07
🎯
PACKAGING

Even the best episode dies without the right packaging. We design thumbnails and titles that stop the scroll, earn the click, and set audience expectations before a single second of audio plays.

  • High-converting custom thumbnails designed for each episode — visually sharp, platform-optimised, and built to outperform generic templates
  • Title engineering using proven frameworks — curiosity gaps, specificity, power words — so your episode ranks and gets clicked
  • Systematic A/B testing of thumbnails and titles to identify what resonates with your specific audience over time
  • Data-driven iteration — we track CTR and performance signals, then refine your packaging strategy episode by episode
07 / 07
✍️
GHOST WRITING

A strong personal brand makes everything easier — better guests say yes faster, your content reaches wider, and your show becomes the one everyone talks about.

  • We study your existing content, tone, and opinions to map your authentic brand voice — your posts sound unmistakably like you
  • Consistent, thoughtful content published on your personal accounts builds the audience you need to attract elite guests
  • Posts are designed to amplify episode reach, cross-promote with guests, and drive listeners back to your show
  • Written content for LinkedIn, X, and newsletter formats — all researched, drafted, and ready to publish
